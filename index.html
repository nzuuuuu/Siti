About Me

Hello! I am Zulaikha, a professional librarian who specializes in Digital Information Services. I help students, researchers, and the public to find, access, and use information effectively in today’s digital world.

With a passion for information science and education, I actively engage in initiatives that promote digital literacy, resource accessibility, and lifelong learning. My mission is to bridge the gap between information and people by leveraging technology and innovation within library services.

  • Created library guides, blog content, and FAQs

Auralis Library Project

I am part of the Auralis Library Project, Malaysia’s first futuristic archive for drama and performing arts. This project merges the traditional values of archiving with cutting-edge digital innovation — including VR drama pods, interactive holograms, and AI-powered curation. I oversee digital resource management, immersive user experience design, and help bring stories to life beyond the screen.
